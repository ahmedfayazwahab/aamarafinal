Where culinary artistry meets the vibrant heritage of India!

Nestled in the heart of Corso Italia, Toronto, Aamara redefines Indian fine dining with a modern and innovative twist. Inspired by the word "Aamara," meaning "eternal" in Sanskrit, our philosophy is to create timeless dining experiences that celebrate the rich culinary traditions of India while embracing global influences.
